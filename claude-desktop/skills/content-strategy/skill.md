# Content Strategy

## Skill Stack

- `brand-strategy` — content pillars must align to brand pillars; messaging must be consistent
- `seo-strategy` — keyword research drives SEO content planning; every article needs a target keyword
- `marketing-strategy` — content is the fuel for marketing channels; align to TOFU/MOFU/BOFU stages
- `data-analytics` — content performance analysis; which content drives traffic, leads, pipeline
- `copywriting` — content brief becomes the input for copywriting; quality of brief determines quality of output
- `deep-research` (ECC) — for thought leadership and research-backed content

## Guardrails

- Content pillars must derive from brand strategy — publishing content that doesn't reinforce positioning creates noise, not authority
- Never build a content calendar without a keyword research pass — content without SEO intent has no compounding value
- Vanity metrics (views, likes, shares) are not success metrics — content success is measured by: leads generated, pipeline influenced, organic traffic, and keyword rankings
- Every piece of content needs a distribution plan before it's created — "publish and hope" is not a strategy
- Content volume without quality destroys brand credibility — one excellent, researched piece outperforms ten shallow ones

## Mode 1: Content Pillar Framework

Content pillars are 3–5 topic areas that represent your brand's expertise and your ICP's needs. Everything you publish lives under a pillar.

**Pillar Selection Process:**
1. What does your brand have credible authority on?
2. What does your ICP most need to know?
3. What content gaps exist in your competitive landscape?
4. Which topics map to high commercial intent keywords?

**Pillar Template:**

```
PILLAR [1–5]: [Topic Name]
─────────────────────────────────────────────
Why this pillar: [How it aligns to brand + ICP need]
Target audience segment: [Who within the ICP]
Funnel stage: [TOFU / MOFU / BOFU / All]
Content formats: [Blog, Video, Podcast, LinkedIn, Email, etc.]
Associated keywords: [Primary keyword cluster]
Business goal: [Lead gen / brand authority / SEO / retention]
Cadence: [X pieces per month]
Success metric: [Organic traffic / leads / newsletter subs / etc.]
```

**Example pillars (SaaS company):**
1. Industry education (TOFU — build audience)
2. Product use cases (MOFU — consideration)
3. Customer success stories (BOFU — conversion)
4. Thought leadership / trends (TOFU — brand authority)
5. How-to / tutorials (MOFU + retention)

## Mode 3: Content Brief Template

Every piece of content (blog, video, email) starts with a brief. A complete brief means the writer needs zero decisions.

```
CONTENT BRIEF
─────────────────────────────────────────────
Title / Headline:
Content type: [Blog / Video / LinkedIn / Email / etc.]
Pillar: [1–5]
Funnel stage: [TOFU / MOFU / BOFU]
Target keyword: [Primary keyword]
Secondary keywords: [2–3 related keywords]
Search intent: [Informational / Commercial / Navigational]
Target word count: [approx]
Target audience: [Specific segment of ICP]

Business goal: [What conversion does this piece drive?]
CTA: [One specific call to action — trial, demo, download, subscribe]

Angle / Hook: [What makes this piece interesting / different from what exists?]

Outline:
H1: [Working title]
  H2: [Section 1]
    - [Key point]
    - [Key point]
  H2: [Section 2]
    - [Key point]
  H2: [Section 3]
  H2: [Conclusion + CTA]

Research required:
- [Stat or study to find]
- [Expert to quote]
- [Competitor article to beat]

Internal links: [Pages on your site to link to]
External links: [Authoritative sources to reference]

SEO notes:
- Target keyword in: H1, first 100 words, 1 H2, image alt text, meta description
- Meta description (150–160 chars):

Tone: [Match brand voice — see brand-strategy]
Deadline: [Date]
Writer: [Name]
```

## Mode 4: SEO Content Planning

**Content-to-Keyword Mapping:**

| Funnel | Keyword Intent | Example Keyword | Content Type |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|-------------|
| TOFU | Informational | "how to reduce customer churn" | Long-form guide |
| TOFU | Informational | "what is NPS score" | Educational article |
| MOFU | Commercial | "best customer retention software" | Comparison page |
| MOFU | Commercial | "[Your Brand] vs [Competitor]" | Competitor comparison |
| BOFU | Transactional | "[Your Brand] pricing" | Pricing page |
| BOFU | Navigational | "[Your Brand] login / tutorial" | Help docs |

**Topic Cluster Model:**
- 1 Pillar Page (comprehensive, 3000+ words) + 8–12 Cluster Articles (supporting, 1000–1500 words)
- All cluster articles link back to pillar page and to each other
- Pillar page ranks for broad keyword; clusters rank for long-tail variations

## Mode 5: Distribution Strategy

Creating content without distributing it = writing in a diary.

**Distribution Matrix:**

| Content Type | Primary Distribution | Secondary | Repurpose Into |
|-------------|---------------------|-----------|----------------|
| Blog post | Organic search | Newsletter, LinkedIn | Short video, LinkedIn carousel, Twitter thread |
| Video | YouTube | Website embed, LinkedIn | Clips for TikTok/IG Reels, blog transcript |
| LinkedIn post | LinkedIn feed | Twitter/X | Email snippet |
| Research / Report | Press, LinkedIn | Email, SlideShare | Blog post series, webinar |
| Podcast episode | Podcast platforms | Website, social | Quote cards, blog summary |
| Case study | Website, sales deck | Email, LinkedIn | Blog post, webinar |

**Content Amplification Checklist:**
- [ ] Email newsletter (existing list — highest conversion)
- [ ] LinkedIn company page + personal pages of team
- [ ] Repurpose for 1–2 short-form formats (Reels, TikTok, Twitter)
- [ ] Internal linking from existing high-traffic pages
- [ ] Outreach to sources quoted in the piece (they may share)
- [ ] Paid promotion for top MOFU/BOFU pieces

## Quality Gate

Before launching a content program:
- [ ] 3–5 content pillars defined and aligned to brand strategy
- [ ] Content calendar built for minimum 8 weeks ahead
- [ ] Every piece has a content brief (not just a topic title)
- [ ] Keyword research completed for SEO content
- [ ] Distribution plan defined per content type
- [ ] Success metrics defined: organic traffic, leads, keyword rankings, newsletter subs
- [ ] Content performance review scheduled (monthly minimum)
- [ ] Repurposing plan exists so each piece generates 3+ outputs
