---

# /bmc

Ask: "Tell me about your business — what you offer, who your customers are, and how you make money."

Then produce:

```markdown
# Business Model Canvas: [Company / Product]
Date: [Date]

---

## 1. Customer Segments
Who are we creating value for?
- [Primary segment: description, size, characteristics]
- [Secondary segment if applicable]

## 2. Value Propositions
What value do we deliver? Why do customers choose us?
- [VP for segment 1]: [what job, what pain, what gain]
- [VP for segment 2]: [what job, what pain, what gain]
Differentiation: [What makes this meaningfully different from alternatives?]

## 3. Channels
How do we reach and deliver to our customers?
- Awareness: [How customers discover us]
- Consideration: [How they evaluate]
- Purchase: [How they buy]
- Delivery: [How they get value]
- After-sales: [How we retain]

## 4. Customer Relationships
What relationship does each segment expect?
- [Segment 1]: [self-service / personal / automated / community]
- [Segment 2]: [relationship type]

## 5. Revenue Streams
How do we generate revenue from each segment?
| Stream | Model | Price | % of Revenue |
|--------|-------|-------|-------------|
| [A] | [subscription/transaction/etc] | $X | X% |
| [B] | | | |

## 6. Key Resources
What assets are essential?
- Physical: [servers, offices, equipment]
- Intellectual: [IP, data, brand, content]
- Human: [key roles, expertise needed]
- Financial: [capital requirements]

## 7. Key Activities
What critical activities must we perform?
- [Activity 1: production / service / platform management]
- [Activity 2]
- [Activity 3]

## 8. Key Partnerships
Who are our key partners and suppliers?
| Partner | What They Provide | Why Critical |
|---------|------------------|-------------|
| [A] | | |
| [B] | | |

## 9. Cost Structure
What are the most significant costs?
| Cost Item | Type | Monthly Est. | % of Total |
|-----------|------|-------------|-----------|
| [A] | Fixed/Variable | $X | X% |
| [B] | Fixed/Variable | $X | X% |

Cost driver: [Cost-driven or Value-driven?]

---

## Unit Economics Summary
- CAC: $X | LTV: $X | LTV:CAC: X:1 | Gross Margin: X%

---

## BMC Gaps & Recommendations
⚠️ [Gap 1: e.g., "Revenue streams are all from one segment — concentration risk"]
⚠️ [Gap 2: e.g., "No owned channel — dependent on paid acquisition"]
💡 [Opportunity: e.g., "Data asset from transactions could be a secondary revenue stream"]
```
