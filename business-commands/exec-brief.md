name: exec-brief
description: Generate an executive briefing, board update, or stakeholder summary. Distills complex situations into clear, decision-ready communication for C-suite or board audiences. Ask for context first.
---

# /exec-brief

Ask:
1. What situation or topic needs a brief?
2. Who is the audience? (CEO / Board / Investors / Customer executive)
3. What decision or action do you need from them?
4. What are the key metrics or data points?

Then produce:

```markdown
# Executive Brief: [Topic]
To: [Audience] | From: [Name] | Date: [Date]
Classification: [Internal / Confidential]

---

## TL;DR
[2–3 sentences. What happened, why it matters, what you need. If they read nothing else, this is enough.]

---

## Situation
[Concise context — no more than 1 paragraph. What's happening and why it's relevant now.]

---

## Key Metrics
| Metric | Value | vs. Last Period | vs. Target |
|--------|-------|----------------|-----------|
| [KPI 1] | | ↑/↓ X% | [On/Off track] |
| [KPI 2] | | ↑/↓ X% | [On/Off track] |
| [KPI 3] | | ↑/↓ X% | [On/Off track] |

---

## What's Working
• [Highlight 1]
• [Highlight 2]
• [Highlight 3]

---

## Challenges
• [Challenge 1] — What we're doing: [mitigation]
• [Challenge 2] — What we're doing: [mitigation]

---

## Decisions Needed
1. [Decision A] — Options: [X / Y] — Recommendation: [X] — Needed by: [Date]
2. [Decision B] — Options: [X / Y] — Recommendation: [Y] — Needed by: [Date]

---

## Top Risks
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| [Risk 1] | H/M/L | H/M/L | [Plan] |

---

## Next Steps
| Action | Owner | Due |
|--------|-------|-----|
| [Action 1] | [Name] | [Date] |
| [Action 2] | [Name] | [Date] |

---

## Appendix (if needed)
[Detailed data, charts, or supporting analysis — only if audience needs to dig in]
```
