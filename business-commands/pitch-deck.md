# /pitch-deck

Ask: "Tell me about your company — what you do, who you serve, your stage, and who you're pitching to."

Then produce:

```markdown
# Pitch Deck: [Company Name]
Audience: [Investors / Board / Partners] | Stage: [Pre-seed/Seed/Series A]

---

## SLIDE 1 — Cover
Company: [Name]
Tagline: [One-line: what you do for whom]
Presenter: [Name, Title]
Contact: [email]

---

## SLIDE 2 — Problem
Headline: [The problem in one bold sentence]
- [Specific pain point 1 with data if available]
- [Specific pain point 2]
- [Who suffers from this and how often]
Opening hook: [A story or scenario that makes the reader feel the pain]

---

## SLIDE 3 — Solution
Headline: [Your solution in one sentence]
- [What it does — in plain language]
- [Key differentiator]
Visual: [Suggest: screenshot / demo gif / before-after]

---

## SLIDE 4 — Market Size
- TAM: $X — [methodology]
- SAM: $X — [our serviceable segment]
- SOM: $X — [realistic 3-year capture]
Beachhead: [Who we're targeting first and why]

---

## SLIDE 5 — Business Model
Revenue model: [subscription / transaction / marketplace / etc.]
Pricing: [Tiers and price points]
Unit economics:
  - CAC: $X
  - LTV: $X
  - LTV:CAC: X:1
  - Payback: X months

---

## SLIDE 6 — Traction
[If available:]
- MRR/ARR: $X (↑X% MoM)
- Customers: X
- Key logos: [List]
- Retention: X%
[If pre-revenue:]
- [Pilots / LOIs / waitlist size / strong signal]
- Why now: [market timing argument]

---

## SLIDE 7 — Competition
Positioning map: [2 axes — describe]
Our position: [Where we sit and why it's defensible]
Key competitors: [Table — us vs. A vs. B on 3–4 dimensions]
Our moat: [What's hard to replicate]

---

## SLIDE 8 — Product Roadmap
Phase 1 (now): [What's built]
Phase 2 (next 6 months): [What's next]
Phase 3 (12+ months): [Where this is going]

---

## SLIDE 9 — Team
[For each founder:]
Name: [X] — Role: [Y]
Why relevant: [1–2 lines on domain expertise / past wins]

Advisors: [Names + 1-line credential]

---

## SLIDE 10 — The Ask
Raising: $X
Round type: [Pre-seed / Seed / Series A]
Use of funds:
  - X% Engineering
  - X% Sales & Marketing
  - X% Operations
Milestones:
  - [What $X gets us to]
  - [Key metric at 18 months]
Timeline: Closing [Month Year]
```
