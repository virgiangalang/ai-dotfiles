---
description: Build a go-to-market plan for a new product, feature, market entry, or relaunch. Covers positioning, target segment, channel strategy, launch timeline, and success metrics.
---

Load the `marketing-strategy` skill, `brand-strategy` skill, and activate the `cmo-advisor` agent.

Build a comprehensive go-to-market plan. Structure:

**Phase 1: Positioning & Audience**
- ICP definition (use marketing-strategy ICP template)
- Positioning statement (use brand-strategy positioning formula)
- Key messages for each audience segment
- Competitive differentiation (what makes this worth switching for?)

**Phase 2: Channel Strategy**
- Which channels for launch? (paid / organic / email / PR / partner / community)
- TOFU/MOFU/BOFU plan — what content/campaign for each stage?
- Launch sequence — what happens before, on, and after launch day?

**Phase 3: Launch Plan**
- T-30 days: [Preparation milestones]
- T-14 days: [Soft launch / beta / early access]
- T-0 (Launch day): [Activities by channel]
- T+7 days: [Follow-up / momentum activities]
- T+30 days: [Performance review + iteration]

**Phase 4: Success Metrics**
- Primary: [The one number that tells us if launch worked]
- Secondary: [Supporting signals]
- Review cadence: [When we evaluate and decide to scale or adjust]

Ask for: product/feature description, target customer, budget range, and timeline before building. Apply the marketing-strategy Quality Gate before delivering.
