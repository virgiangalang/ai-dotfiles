description: Build a complete integrated marketing campaign brief — objective, target audience, messaging, creative direction, channel plan, budget, timeline, and success metrics.
---

Load the `marketing-strategy` skill, `copywriting` skill, and activate the `cmo-advisor` agent.

Build a complete integrated campaign brief. Include every section:

**Campaign Overview**
- Campaign name
- Campaign goal (1 primary objective: leads / revenue / awareness / retention)
- Target audience (ICP segment for this campaign)
- Campaign duration (start → end date)
- Total budget

**Messaging**
- Core message (the one thing we want the audience to remember)
- Primary benefit (what's in it for them)
- Offer (what are we giving them to take action?)
- CTA (one specific action — sign up / book demo / download / buy)

**Creative Direction**
- Tone (aligned to brand voice)
- Visual direction (mood, imagery style)
- Key visual formats needed (social static / video / email / landing page / ads)
- Content hooks (3 angles to test)

**Channel Plan**
| Channel | Format | Budget % | Target metric | Owner |
|---------|--------|---------|--------------|-------|
| [channel] | [format] | X% | [CPL/ROAS/reach] | |

**Timeline**
- Brief approved: [date]
- Creative due: [date]
- Campaign live: [date]
- Mid-campaign review: [date]
- Campaign end + debrief: [date]

**Success Metrics**
- Primary: [metric + target]
- Secondary: [2–3 supporting metrics]
- Guardrails: [metrics we must not worsen — brand sentiment, unsubscribe rate, etc.]
