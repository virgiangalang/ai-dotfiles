Load the `growth-metrics` skill and activate the `growth-marketer` agent.

Conduct a complete growth and funnel audit. Deliver:
1. **Funnel Snapshot** — conversion rates at each step:
   - Visitor → Lead (CPL)
   - Lead → MQL (MQL rate)
   - MQL → SQL (MQL-to-SQL rate)
   - SQL → Customer (close rate)
   - Overall visitor-to-customer conversion

2. **Unit Economics Health**:
   - CAC (blended + by channel)
   - LTV and LTV:CAC ratio
   - Payback period
   - Monthly churn rate

3. **Channel Performance** — CAC and ROAS by channel, ranked by efficiency

4. **Biggest Drop-off** — identify the single highest-leverage conversion problem with root cause hypothesis

5. **Top 3 Growth Experiments** — for each:
   - Hypothesis (If X, then Y, because Z)
   - Primary metric
   - Duration
   - Effort estimate (Low / Medium / High)
   - Expected impact

Apply all formulas from the growth-metrics skill. Ask for current data before diagnosing; do not assume numbers.
