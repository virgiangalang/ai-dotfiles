Load the `email-marketing` skill and `copywriting` skill. Activate the `copywriter` agent.

Write a complete email sequence. Ask for: sequence type (welcome / nurture / re-engagement), audience segment, goal (trial / demo / purchase / re-engagement), brand voice, and any existing customer language / testimonials to draw from.

For each email in the sequence, deliver:

**Email [N] — [Type: Welcome / Value / Story / Social Proof / Offer]**
- Timing: [Send timing — immediately / Day X]
- Goal: [What this email achieves]
- Subject line options (3 variants):
  - A: [Subject] — Type: [Curiosity / Result / Direct / Question]
  - B: [Subject]
  - C: [Subject]
  - RECOMMENDED: [A/B/C] — because [reason]
- Preview text: [90 chars]
- Body: [Full email copy — formatted, ready to paste]
- CTA: [Button text] — [Link target]
- Notes: [A/B test suggestion, personalization tokens]

Apply the email sequence templates from the email-marketing skill:
- Welcome sequence: 5 emails over 10 days
- Nurture sequence: 6–8 emails over 3–4 weeks (70/30 value/offer mix)
- Re-engagement: 2-email sequence with suppress logic

Apply Quality Gate from email-marketing skill before delivering. Check deliverability requirements (unsubscribe link, physical address, consent language).
