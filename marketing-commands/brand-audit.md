description: Audit brand consistency, clarity, and differentiation across all touchpoints. Delivers a prioritized fix list.
---

Load the `brand-strategy` skill and activate the `brand-strategist` agent.

Conduct a comprehensive brand audit. Evaluate:
1. **Consistency** — is the same message used across website, social, email, and sales materials?
2. **Clarity** — can a first-time visitor identify the ICP and core value prop within 5 seconds of landing on the homepage?
3. **Differentiation** — does the positioning differ meaningfully from the top 3 competitors? Run the competitor test.
4. **Voice & Tone** — is the brand voice consistent? Flag any off-brand copy.
5. **Messaging Hierarchy** — does a clear hierarchy exist from brand promise → pillars → proof points?

Deliver findings with severity levels (CRITICAL / HIGH / MEDIUM) and a prioritized fix list. Apply the Brand Audit Checklist from the brand-strategy skill.
